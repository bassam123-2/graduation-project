Founder & President

Dr. Mohammad Khaled Al-Tamimi

Dr. Mohammad Khaled Al-Tamimi is a distinguished specialist in physical therapy and rehabilitation, with a PhD in Physical Therapy. He is the founder and president of the Elaji Integrated Center, and a member of several international medical associations. Dr. Al-Tamimi has contributed to the development of modern rehabilitation protocols and has participated in numerous conferences and workshops. He is dedicated to providing the highest standards of care and innovation in the field.
