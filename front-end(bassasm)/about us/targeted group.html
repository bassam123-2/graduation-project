Our Target Groups

Comprehensive Rehabilitation Services for Various Medical Conditions

At AL-BOQAI Center, we provide specialized rehabilitation and physical therapy services for a wide range of medical conditions and patient groups. Our expert team is equipped to handle complex cases and provide personalized treatment plans for each individual.

Target Groups

  • Oxygen deficiency, cerebral palsy, and all types of paralysis.
  • Post-accident injuries.
  • All types of strokes.
  • All types of dystrophy.
  • Multiple sclerosis.
  • All disabilities that affect a normal person's life. Here, disability means any problem that hinders the life of a normal person.
  • Cases that have been diagnosed as incurable.
  • Joint and spinal problems.
  • Migraine, headache, constipation (soft tissue problems).
  • Spinal cord injuries.
  • All types of birth dislocations.
  • Epilepsy.
  • Musculoskeletal system problems.

Our Approach

We believe that every patient deserves personalized care and attention. Our comprehensive approach combines advanced therapeutic techniques with compassionate care to help patients achieve their maximum potential for recovery and improved quality of life.

Specialized Care

Our team of experienced therapists specializes in treating complex medical conditions and works closely with patients and their families to develop effective treatment plans that address both physical and emotional needs.
